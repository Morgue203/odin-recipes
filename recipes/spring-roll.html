Vietnamese Spring Roll

Description

Vietnamese spring rolls are one of the most popular dishes that you will see in any region of Vietnam.

Spring Roll Ingredients

  • 2 ounces dried thin rice noodles
  • 3/4 cup ground chicken
  • 1/4 cup shrimp - washed, peeled, and cut into small pieces
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 4 wood fungus mushroom, chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1.2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 24 rice paper wrappers
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft, about 20 minutes. Drain well, cut into 2-inch long pieces.

  3. Step 3

    4. Combine noodles pieces, chicken, shrimp, eggs, carrot, wood fungus mushroom, and green onions in a large bowl. Add sugar, salt, and black pepper, stir well.

  4. Step 4

    5. Soak 1 rice paper wrapper in a shallow bowl of warm water to soften, about 15 seconds. Place on a damp cloth laid out a flat surface.

  5. Step 5

    6. Place 1 tablespoon filling mixture into the center of softened rice paper. Fold the bottom edge into the center, covering filling. Fold in opposing edges and roll up tightly. Repeat with remaining rice paper wrappers, soaking and filling each one individually.

  6. Step 6

    7. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.

  7. Step 7

    8. Working in batches of 3 or 4, fry spring roll in hot oil until crisp and golden brown on both sides, about 5 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
